Join Us

The Campaign for Gender Inclusivity is always looking for new members! If you are a teen, LGBTQ+ or not, we’d love to have you! No experience with community organizing is required. By joining C4GI, you’ll have opportunities to create educational social media content, find events for us to fundraise and share our mission, curate resources for faculty and students, host LGBTQ+ support groups, and more! Joining C4GI is a great way to meet passionate and like-minded individuals and form new relationships. Check out how you can get involved below!


What You Can Do

Join Our NJ Chapter

C4GI is based in New Jersey, where we were founded. Our NJ chapter is our most active, and we're always looking for new members! If you're a teen interested in creating change and living in the NJ area, join us by filling out this form, and we'll get in touch!

Join Our India Chapter

We also have a thriving chapter in India! If you live in India, fill out this form to join our India Chapter. You can also find them on Instagram, under the handle @c4gi_india.

Start Your Own Chapter

Don't live close to any of our existing chapters? Start your own! Just fill out this form and we'll get in touch! No experience in community organizing is required, just reach out!

Help Out Virtually

Don't live in the area, but don't have the capacity to start your own hub? Don't despair, you can still join C4GI! We have lots of virtual opportunities and events. Shoot us an email and we'll work something out. We'd love to hear from you!
