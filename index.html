The Campaign For Gender Inclusivity (C4GI)

The Campaign for Gender Inclusivity (C4GI) is a student led organization that provides support for transgender and non-binary youth to advocate for themselves in schools by hosting student workshops and sharing resources, training teachers on inclusivity in the classroom, and fundraising for charities that align with our mission.

Join C4GI

The Campaign for Gender Inclusivity is always looking for new members! If you are a teen, LGBTQ+ or not, we’d love to have you! No experience with community organizing is required. By joining C4GI, you’ll have opportunities to create educational social media content, find events for us to fundraise and share our mission, curate resources for faculty and students, host LGBTQ+ support groups, and more! Joining C4GI is a great way to meet passionate and like-minded individuals and form new relationships. If you have any questions, don’t hesitate to reach out!

40% of LGBTQ respondents seriously considered attempting suicide in the past twelve months, with more than half of transgender and nonbinary youth having seriously considered suicide

86% of LGBTQ youth said that recent politics have negatively impacted their well-being
